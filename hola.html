About Us

Connect with Respect is an organization dedicated to helping those affected by cyberbullying.

We aim to educate and offer resources to fight against digital harassment.

What is Cyberbullying?

Cyberbullying refers to bullying over digital platforms such as social media, texts, and gaming.

It can happen to anyone, but it is particularly harmful to young people who may not yet have the tools to cope with online harassment.
