About Us

Connect with Respect is an organization dedicated to helping individuals who are victims of cyberbullying. We understand the damaging effects of online harassment and its impact on mental health. Our mission is to provide a safe space for victims to share their experiences, seek support, and get advice on how to handle the emotional and psychological challenges caused by cyberbullying.

We believe that no one should suffer in silence, and our goal is to raise awareness about the seriousness of cyberbullying, as well as to offer resources and guidance for those who need help. Our team of trained professionals is committed to providing confidential support and guidance for anyone who reaches out to us.

Together, we can make the internet a safer place for everyone, and we invite you to join us in our efforts to stop cyberbullying.

About Us

Connect with respect is an organization that helps each and every person who needs psychological help or just moral support, knowing that they are listening to you and supporting you in whatever you need. It is an anonymous organization; you do not have to reveal who you are or who is "harassing" or "bullying" you. If you give us your consent, we can make your experience as a victim of cyberbullying known (obviously anonymously).
