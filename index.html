About Us

Connect with Respect is an organization that helps individuals suffering from cyberbullying. In addition to providing support, it offers them the opportunity to share their experiences anonymously. While it may not always be recognized, suicide rates related to cyberbullying increased by 7.6% from 2021 to 2022, highlighting the urgent need for action. That is why Eduardo Alvarado Treviño, Jorge Zazueta Soto, Matías Lasprilla, and Lucas Ramos are committed to preventing and supporting victims of this issue.

More information...

Cyberbullying is a form of bullying that occurs through digital platforms such as social media, text messaging, or online gaming. It involves using technology to harass, threaten, embarrass, or target individuals or groups. This type of bullying can manifest in various ways, such as sending abusive messages, impersonating someone to damage their reputation, sharing private information without consent, excluding someone from online discussions, and publicly humiliating victims by sharing embarrassing content.

The effects of cyberbullying can be severe, leading to anxiety, depression, and even suicidal thoughts. Victims often experience feelings of isolation and powerlessness, which can exacerbate mental health problems. It is crucial to raise awareness of this issue and take proactive steps to combat it.
